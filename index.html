About me

@Awfulnaut

I started getting my feet wet with web development when the college I was attending needed a new online platform for publishing the wealth of art generated by the surrounding community. I shifted my attention from my Biology degree—which has done little other than collect dust—to making baby steps in web dev. Pythia Journal was born! And then quickly died due to lack of continued support... but it was enough to pique my interest!

Now, I work as a front end web developer for Digital Wave building websites and web apps for clients in higher ed, not-for-profit, and the medical industry. I live for code reviews, well-commented code, and working with design to create clean, intuitive products. When I'm not coding, you can bet that I'm either playing or working on my current pastime: Beat Saber ( check out my custom charts)!
